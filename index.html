About ICEFOSS

ICE-FOSS (ICEFOSS is a Conference for Enthusiasts of Free and Open Source Software) is Free Software Conference organized by Fisat Free Software Cell (FFSC).

ICEFOSS 2017, scheduled on August 25,26 2016 includes Workshops and Talks covering different aspects of Free Software. ICEFOSS 2017 is the sixth edition of this conference.

  • About FISAT

Federal Institute of Science And Technology (FISAT)® was established in 2002 under the aegis of Federal Bank Officers' Association Educational Society (FBOAES).Federal Institute of Science And Technology (FISAT) has a unique position in the Professional Education Sector in South India.

The college has carved a niche for itself in education world, eloquently demonstrated by the flying colors attained by its students in academics, placements as well as in extra curricular and co-curricular activities.
